The old sea-dogs say that for nearly a century these waters have been home to mysterious storms and whirlpools. 
Dozens of ships, laden with treasure, have gone to the bottom of the ocean! Over the years many adventurers have 
tried to find this precious booty. But these waters swarm with vicious creatures: tigersharks, hammerheads, ghost 
sharks, and worse! Now, it's your turn to dive...
